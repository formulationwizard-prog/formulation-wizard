## Methodology
Ranked by commercial frequency (how often the ingredient appears in real supplement products):
- **Tier 1 — Universal core (43):** the canonical SKU form of each DV-established vitamin & mineral (with up to 2–3 very-common forms where the form itself is ubiquitous — e.g. Magnesium Glycinate + Citrate + Oxide). Every multivitamin/mineral carries these.

Every entry below is a **verbatim catalog name**. `⚠` = carrier-loaded SKU (`potencyFactor < 1`) — these are the near-zero-active-guard targets and the carrier-loaded-polish priority set.
